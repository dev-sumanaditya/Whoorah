This video is a satire on society's prejudices about women, and how they always assume the worst in every woman. We live in a world where if there's an unsettling situation, it's a girl who is always blamed for it, whether it is a situation where a woman is evolved or even those where it's not even about a woman but people always assume first that it's about a woman. This video hits hard on this mentality of our society in a comic and satirical way.
